Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about BMW Premium Selection

What is BMW Premium Selection?

BMW Premium Selection is our certified pre-owned program that offers thoroughly inspected, premium quality pre-owned BMW vehicles with comprehensive warranty coverage and additional benefits that provide peace of mind.

What inspection process do Premium Selection vehicles undergo?

Every BMW Premium Selection vehicle undergoes a comprehensive multi-point inspection by BMW-certified technicians. This includes mechanical components, electrical systems, interior and exterior condition, and road testing to ensure the vehicle meets BMW's stringent quality standards.

What warranty coverage comes with a Premium Selection vehicle?

All BMW Premium Selection vehicles come with a minimum 12-month warranty with unlimited mileage coverage. This comprehensive warranty covers a wide range of components and includes BMW Roadside Assistance for added peace of mind.

Can I finance or lease a BMW Premium Selection vehicle?

Yes, BMW Financial Services offers attractive financing and leasing options specifically tailored for Premium Selection vehicles. Our financial advisors can help you find a payment plan that suits your budget and preferences.

Is there a return policy if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

BMW Premium Selection offers a 14-day/1,000 km exchange privilege. If you're not completely satisfied with your vehicle within this period, you can exchange it for another BMW Premium Selection vehicle of equal or greater value (subject to terms and conditions).
